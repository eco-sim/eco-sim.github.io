TextWizardPro

Your native solution to read text from your photos in bulk.

Unleash Trapped Text

TextWizardPro allows you to loop through images natively on your computer and uses OCR technology to convert words found in images into a text file for you to use. It works with almost any image file type, including PDFs. It can save hours of hand by hand translation, without the hassle of uploading photos. What are you waiting for?

Whether it's 4 photos or 400, TextWizardPro can tackle it. Can you imagine having to go through are your photos manually and converting them to text? Or uploading all those photos to a sketchy online service, and then dowloading all of the results? TextWizardPro is not only less chaos, but more privacy and security.
